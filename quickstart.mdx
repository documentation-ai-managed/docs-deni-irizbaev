## Create Your Account and Log In

Sign up for Deni Irizbaev in under a minute. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.deni-irizbaev.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, name, and a secure password. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and click **Log In**. You'll land on your personalized dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Default Space

Every Deni Irizbaev account starts with a default documentation space. Configure it to match your project needs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Spaces" icon="folder">
    From the sidebar, select **Spaces** > **Default Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Update the space name (e.g., "My Project Docs"), add a description, and set visibility to **Public** or **Private**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Click **Update Space**. Your default space is now ready for projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Project

Build your initial project using MDX for rich, interactive docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    In your default space, click **New Project** and name it "Quickstart Guide".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Page" icon="file-text">
    Select **New Page**, title it "Introduction", and choose the MDX template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Paste sample MDX content and preview it live.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Introduction
    description: Welcome to your docs.
    ---

    ## Hello World

    This is your first MDX page!
    ````
    ````markdown
    # Introduction

    Welcome to your Markdown page.
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Click **Publish** to make it live at `https://deni-irizbaev.com/docs/quickstart`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    ````
    Go to your dashboard and click "New Project".
    Enter the project name and summary.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first page">
    ````
    Click "Add Page" in the sidebar.
    Name your page and start entering documentation.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    ````
    Open "Settings" > "Team" to invite others by email.
    Permissions update instantly.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Symptoms

- Login page keeps reloading
- "Access Denied" message shown

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Try clearing cache/cookies and reload `https://deniirizbaev.com`.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permission">
    ````
    Ensure your email is added to the project by the admin.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try incognito/private mode">
    ````
    If it works, a browser extension may be blocking login.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
